About

ORDII was originally an inscription protocol based on the Bitcoin network. However, excessive writing caused congestion in the Bitcoin network and prolonged packaging time. In fact, this protocol is full of controversy. Therefore, in order to solve this problem, we developed ORDII based on the bsc network, which is extremely fast and efficient, has high throughput, and has extremely low gas transfer fees, making it suitable for large-scale holdings and transactions.
